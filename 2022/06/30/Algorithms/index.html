    Scientific method applied to analysis of algorithms_A framework for predicting performance and comparing algorithms.

    Method

    Observe some feature of the natural world.

    Hypothesize a model that is consistent with the observations. Predict events using the hypothesis.

    Verify the predictions by making further observations.

    Validate by repeating until the hypothesis and observations agree.

    Principles
    • Experiments must be reproducible.
    • Hypotheses must be falsifiable.
